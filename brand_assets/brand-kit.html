Logo Usage Guidelines

  • Minimum size: 40px height
  • Always use on solid backgrounds (black or white)
  • Maintain clear space equal to 1/4 of the mark's width on all sides
  • Do not stretch, distort, or rotate the logo
  • Use white on dark backgrounds, black on light backgrounds

Typography Standards

  • Display headings: Cormorant Garamond, weight 600, tracking -0.03em
  • Body text: Inter, weight 400, line-height 1.7
  • Minimum font size for body: 16px on mobile, 16px on desktop
  • Headings on mobile should be reduced 30-40% from desktop
  • Line length should not exceed 75 characters for optimal readability

Brand Guidelines

● Logo Placement

  • Primary position: top-left or center of layouts
  • Always maintain clear space (1/4 of mark width)
  • Use solid background: black (#1A1A1A) or white (#FFFFFF)
  • Never place on textured or photographic backgrounds
  • Minimum reproduction size: 40px height

● Voice & Tone

  • Professional yet approachable
  • Emphasize quality and attention to detail
  • Confident without being arrogant
  • Use clear, direct language (avoid jargon)
  • Highlight craftsmanship and experience

● Spacing & Layout

  • Use consistent spacing tokens throughout
  • Desktop sections: 120-160px vertical padding
  • Mobile sections: 60-80px vertical padding
  • Create intentional visual hierarchy with whitespace
  • Grid-based layouts for visual coherence

● Interactive States

  • Hover: +4px transform lift, color shift to accent blue
  • Focus-visible: accent border, clear outline
  • Active: subtle scale down, color darkening
  • Disabled: reduced opacity (50%), no interaction
  • Transitions: use transform & opacity only (no transition-all)

Photography & Imagery

Our photography style showcases architectural excellence and refined living spaces. Images should highlight craftsmanship, materiality, and the transformative power of our work.

Photography Style

  • High contrast compositions with strong lighting
  • Clean, architectural perspectives
  • Focus on material detail and texture
  • Natural light preferred; minimize harsh shadows
  • Consistent color grading (neutral to warm tones)
  • People (when featured) should appear natural and contemplative

Image Treatments

  • Gradient overlay: bg-gradient-to-t from-black/60
  • Color treatment: blend-multiply for muted, sophisticated tone
  • Aspect ratios: 16:9 for hero images, 4:3 for cards
  • Never allow images to overflow viewport on mobile
  • Scale proportionally across breakpoints

Subject Matter

  • Interior spaces: living areas, kitchens, bedrooms, bathrooms
  • Exterior facades and landscaping details
  • Poolside areas and outdoor entertainment spaces
  • Construction process (refined, not chaotic)
  • Material close-ups: wood, stone, tile, concrete finishes

What to Avoid

  • Overly saturated or unrealistic colors
  • Cluttered or poorly composed shots
  • Low-quality or blurry images
  • Stock photography (appearance of generic content)
  • Over-processed or heavily filtered looks

Mobile Requirements

  • Navigation: hamburger menu or bottom navigation on screens < 768px
  • Typography: reduce heading sizes 30-40% from desktop
  • Body text: minimum 16px for readability
  • Touch targets: all clickable elements 44x44px minimum
  • Images: scale down proportionally, never overflow viewport
  • Horizontal scroll: strictly prohibited
